01

You already use AI every day

AI is not science fiction. It quietly powers services you rely on — often without you noticing.

🎬

Netflix recommendations

Suggests the next show by learning from what millions of viewers watch.

✉️

Email spam filtering

Sorts junk from real mail by spotting patterns in billions of messages.

🗺️

Maps & routing

Predicts traffic and the fastest route using live and historic data.

🛡️

Fraud detection

Flags unusual card activity in milliseconds to protect your money.

Human role

People decide which problems are worth solving and set the rules these everyday systems must follow.

What actually makes it “AI”?

Almost every AI system follows the same simple loop. Learn this once and you understand them all.

📊

Data

Examples to learn from

🧠

Model

Spots the patterns

🎯

Prediction

Makes a best guess

✅

Outcome

An action or answer

🔁

Feedback

Learns & improves

Human role

Humans choose the data, judge if predictions are fair and useful, and provide the feedback that makes the model better.

09

Why human oversight matters

AI is powerful but imperfect. These are the things people must keep watching — especially in public service.

🔒

Privacy

Protect personal data and use it only as allowed.

⚖️

Bias

Make sure outcomes are fair to every group.

🎯

Accuracy

Check the answer is actually correct.

🛟

Safety

Prevent harm to people and services.

🛡️

Security

Keep systems and data protected from misuse.

📋

Accountability

Know who is responsible for each decision.

Human role

Oversight is not optional. Someone must always be answerable for what AI does.

10

How everyday work will change

The shift is from doing every task by hand to supervising, checking and improving AI-supported work.

Today — manual

  • Typing the same replies
  • Sorting forms by hand
  • Searching long documents
  • Repetitive data entry
➜

Tomorrow — supervising

  • Reviewing AI drafts
  • Checking accuracy & fairness
  • Handling tricky exceptions
  • Improving how AI is used

Worker tasks

More reviewing and judging, less repetition.

Customer interactions

Faster answers, with people for the hard moments.

Partner engagement

Better data sharing and quicker collaboration.

Human role

Your business knowledge becomes more valuable — you guide the AI, it doesn't guide you.

11

New roles AI is creating

AI doesn't only remove tasks — it creates new, very human jobs that need judgement and care.

🧭

AI product owner

Decides what AI should and shouldn't do for the service.

🔍

AI assurance lead

Checks AI is safe, fair and working as intended.

🗃️

Data steward

Looks after data quality, privacy and permissions.

📈

Automation analyst

Finds the best tasks to automate and measures the value.

✍️

Prompt / workflow designer

Shapes how people and AI work together, step by step.

Human role

These are careers built on human strengths — context, ethics and good judgement.

The one thing to remember

AI automates tasks — but humans provide judgement, trust and purpose.
