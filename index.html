10

How everyday work will change

The shift is from doing every task by hand to supervising, checking and improving AI-supported work.

Today — manual

  • Typing the same replies
  • Sorting forms by hand
  • Searching long documents
  • Repetitive data entry
➜

Tomorrow — supervising

  • Reviewing AI drafts
  • Checking accuracy & fairness
  • Handling tricky exceptions
  • Improving how AI is used

Worker tasks

More reviewing and judging, less repetition.

Customer interactions

Faster answers, with people for the hard moments.

Partner engagement

Better data sharing and quicker collaboration.

The one thing to remember

AI automates tasks — but humans provide judgement, trust and purpose.
